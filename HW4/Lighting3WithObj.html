Please use a browser that supports "canvas"
Scale:

Camera controls:

  • w, a, s, d - move forward, left, back, right
  • W, A - zoom in, zoom out (changes field of view)
  • r, f - move up, down
  • i, j, k, l - look up, turn left, look down, turn right
  • O - look at origin
  • I, J, K, L - orbit up, left, down, right (must be facing origin!)

Model controls:

  • n - toggle face normals or vertex normals
  • t, T - increase/decrease specular exponent
  • SPACE - pause rotation
  • y - rotate about y axis
  • x - rotate about x axis
  • z - rotate about z axis
  • o - Reset to initial state
