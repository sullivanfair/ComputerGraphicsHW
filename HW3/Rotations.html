Keyboard controls:
  • d - rotate camera 15 degrees around y-axis
  • D - rotate camera direction -15 degrees around y-axis
  • e - toggle camera elevation up/down
  • y - RotateY(incr) (denoted "Y")
  • Y - RotateY(-incr) (denoted "Y-1")
  • x - RotateX(incr)
  • X - RotateX(-incr)
  • z - RotateZ(incr)
  • Z - RotateZ(-incr)
  • o - Reset to identity
  • p, q, r - undo effects of RotateZ(-45) * RotateY(-45) (works in intrinsic mode only)
    (make sure the correct code block is uncommented in Rotations.js)
